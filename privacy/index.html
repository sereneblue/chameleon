Privacy Policy

Chameleon doesn't collect any information from users.

ipapi.co is no longer used for geolocation as of v0.21.18.
A Vercel app now powers IP geolocation.
If you are using a specific feature (language or timezone spoofing), a request may be made to ipapi.co. This functionality is only enabled when the user specfies "IP" for those features. ipapi's privacy policy can be found here.
